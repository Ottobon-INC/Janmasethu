Stage 1: Thinking of Parenthood

Emotions: Hopeful, anxious, uncertain (“Are we ready?”).

Pain Points:

Social/family pressure.

Financial worries.

Nutrition & lifestyle confusion.

Myths vs facts about fertility.

Sakhi’s Role:

Gentle Q&A (“What should I think about before trying?”).

Curated NGO fact sheets localized (nutrition, age factors).

Financial planning checklists.

Emotional reassurance content.

Stage 2: Trying Naturally

Emotions: Optimistic, but quickly anxious if results don’t come.

Pain Points:

Cycle tracking is confusing.

Conflicting online advice (“eat X, avoid Y”).

Pressure increases with each month.

Sakhi’s Role:

Simple cycle tracker with educational prompts.

Nutrition & lifestyle reminders.

Reframing vague worries into clear questions (“What lifestyle changes actually impact fertility?”).

Mental health micro-content (stress reduction).

Stage 3: Exploring Options (IUI, IVF, Donor)

Emotions: Overwhelmed, shame, decision fatigue.

Pain Points:

Don’t know when to see a specialist.

Complex choices (IUI vs IVF vs donor).

Clinic processes unclear.

High financial stakes.

Sakhi’s Role:

Preparedness checklists (“What to ask at first consult”).

Curated video snippets from popular doctors (with context).

Document uploader for tests.

Gentle “Send to my clinic” hand-off (opt-in).

FAQs localized: costs, success rates, process explained.

Stage 4: During Treatment (IVF/IUI)

Emotions: Stressed, vulnerable, fearful of failure.

Pain Points:

Medication schedules complicated.

Procedures unfamiliar.

Fear of failure and blame.

Sakhi’s Role:

Medication reminders (WhatsApp/SMS).

Stage-wise explainer content (“What happens on Day 3”).

Asynchronous nurse inbox for non-urgent clarifications.

Preparedness score (checklist completion).

Stage 5: Pregnancy

Emotions: Relief, excitement, but new anxieties.

Pain Points:

Trimester-specific guidance is fragmented.

Social myths vs clinical advice.

Financial planning for delivery.

Sakhi’s Role:

Trimester timeline with curated content.

Nutrition checklists per stage.

Embedded videos (“What to expect in second trimester”).

“Ask better questions” prompts for OBGYN visits.

Stage 6: Delivery & Post-Delivery

Emotions: Joy, exhaustion, overwhelm.

Pain Points:

Post-partum recovery support lacking.

Infant care myths vs facts.

Mental health stigma (post-partum depression).

Sakhi’s Role:

Post-delivery care checklist (mother + baby).

Curated mental health NGO resources.

Baby immunization reminders.

“Ask-a-nurse” extended to newborn FAQs.
